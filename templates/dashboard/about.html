Mission

Helping girls learn together across Dominica

Study_girl is an MVP for Wesley High School students. It connects girls who need help in CSEC subjects with girls who are stronger in those subjects, while keeping safety and encouragement at the center.

Python-first

Django keeps the app readable for students who have learned Python in IT class.

Realistic live study

WebRTC handles peer-to-peer media. WebSockets handle signaling, presence, chat, and whiteboard drawing.

Honest focus mode

The app can encourage fullscreen and detect tab changes, but it cannot block phone notifications or operating-system alerts.
